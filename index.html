Chitwan - A Strange Town

Chitwan, the name I listened from my father mouth when we had to move there. I was just litte boy of 7 when our family got here where we were unknown to all. But, that how we can make new friends,new friendly neighbours right? We stayed in the place called krishnapur(near Cancer Hospital) where, we had to rent a home as our quater was under construction. After some years,we shifted to Cancer Hospital - better life started then, I met many friends who were much more friendly than I thought. 2000's times had the best picture because we had the real way to make our life happy and I am glad that I was the part of 2000's AD. I still remember that Mobile phones were not a thing, Internet was dial-up and Computer were used for paint and play MARIO,DAV and GTA II/III mostly. And you already know about today's lifestyle. At 10 I eventually got a suprise gift - A bicycle at my birthday. The happiness was indescribable. My life then got intresting as we the friends used to go around the Hospital then gather to play football cricket many more. I had to say I enjoyed spending my times more with Hospital friends than school friends.
So,this was my short and sweet version of chitwan diaries which was sometime an strange place which became my hometown.


Mustang Trek - The Trip

Now, thats what I call the best trip that I had been till now. The soothing wind blowing our mind and heart away.
The excitement started, when we got on the scropio (jeep) - the way was bumpy with ups and downs throughout, but the flowing Kali Gandaki river along with us added just the perfect delight. After some hours, its like we landed into a dreamland with Mount Dhaulagiri range shining bright like diamond, the crystal clear sky and the cool breeze that swept us off our feets. We then reached Jomsom after getting us some place to stay we freshened up and started with having a stroll around the city.The warm and welcoming smile of those people we met on our way provided us with sense of belonging. The day tommorrow - Morning having tea in a place surrounded by the hills and himalayas,the soothing start of a day. The trek started as we travelled through the track finally reaching the Muktinath Temple located at the foot of the Thorong La mountain pass. Then, there comes a sort of challenging part where, we have to run over the 108 holy tap-water with the floor being frozen ice and water being cold as hell also diving in the pond full of cold water as there was an ancient saying that "Our every sins will be forgotten once we completed these tests". We dared and completed the so called holy task . So, who can forget such a moment as you have lived these moment to fullest without any worries and tension.
Haven't you visited??Just go there and live your life.
